Who am I?

Mostly known as serchpics on Instagram, Sergio (which is me) is an 18 year old photographer currently based in Dublin, Ireland, but born in Spain, Madrid, where I sometimes reside for periods of time.

With the World as my workplace, I pursue a wide variety of photography styles but center my attention in portraits, landscapes, cars and animals (wild life and pets).

My interest in this fine art started in the summer of 2018, and since then photography as been part of my life. Initially (and for a long time) it was just another one of my hobbies, but with time it has become more important to the point of doing proffesional work.

From portraits and car sessions to taking pictures of families and their pets, I am able to perform in a range of areas. Also, during my years as a photographer I've mastered the art of editing photos using Adobe Lightroom, and this service has also been hired in a number of ocassions.

As for my gear, I use the Sony Alpha 6400 with two different lenses: the Sony 18-135 mm, f-3.5/5.6 and the Sony E 35mm F1.8 OSS.

Also, this helps to create a more relaxed and friendly atmosphere, which is what I look for when working with my clients. You can bring as many props and outfits as you want and we will work together with whatever we have to get the best portraits possible.

The price is 40€ for 50 minutes of actual photography, and it also includes the careful selection and edition of the pictures with the proffesional software Lighroom.
